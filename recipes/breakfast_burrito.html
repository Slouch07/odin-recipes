Tropical Burrito

Description

Scrambled eggs, bacon, cheese, and a hashbrown patty are served up in a tortilla for a satisfying start into your day.

Ingredients

  • 2 strips bacon, or more to taste
  • 1 frozen hashbrown patty
  • 2 eggs, beaten, or more to taste
  • 1/4 cup shredded Cheddar and Swiss cheese mixture
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 (12-Inch) flour tortilla
